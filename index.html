  • Discussion
  • How has your baptism impacted your faith journey and relationship with God?
  • How has your relationship with God evolved since your baptism? In what ways do you feel closer to God now?
  • How can we, as a small group, support and pray for each other in our faith journeys post-baptism?
  • How do you feel called to live out your faith and fulfill your purpose as a baptized believer?
  • Discuss any challenges or doubts you've encountered since your baptism. How did you overcome or address them?
  • What are your thoughts on the role of baptism in salvation and the forgiveness of sins?
  • Baptism often involves making commitments or vows. Are there specific commitments you made during your baptism that you feel called to renew or focus on in your current season of life?
  • What advice or encouragement would you offer to someone considering baptism or to a newly baptized believer based on your own experiences?
